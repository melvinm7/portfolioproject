site description.

The users of my site are job recruiters, or people who want to look at my work or portfolio.

I want the users to be able to see my skills and get to know me better as an individual.

I want to solve the problem of any recruiters having questions of who I am, or if they question if I am able to show skills that are portrayed on my resume.

After looking through my website, I want job recruiters to contact me and reach out to me with offers, so I am able to receive a job.
